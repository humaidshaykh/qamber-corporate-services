ABOUT US

Qamber Corporate Services is an experienced business setup consultant in Dubai. Our experts help guide and assist you in all kinds of business setup-related services in Dubai, as per your requirements. Our mission is to provide the best PRO and business setup services to our clients across various industry verticals in the UAE and the GCC region.
We, at GlobalLink, work alongside clients to simplify the path for the formation and maintenance of a business in Dubai mainland or Free Zones. With over 16 years of industry experience and a team of highly experienced professionals, we help entrepreneurs and investors curate their business journeys.
We are your trusted and reliable business setup consultant in Dubai. We offer a range of inter-related services, whether you need a trade license, visa, or need help with other rules and regulations governing business management in the UAE.
